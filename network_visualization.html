Campus Organization Network Analysis

This interactive visualization shows the connections between different campus organizations at Wellesley based on shared membership. The organizations analyzed include:

  • WZLY - campus radio station
  • Whiptails - ultimate frisbee team
  • HKSA - Hong Kong Student Association
  • Glass Garage - maker space
  • Data Science 27 - academic group

How to interact: Hover over nodes to see details, pan by clicking and dragging, zoom with your mouse wheel or trackpad.
